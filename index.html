May, 2014

Design of an Ultra Wide Band Antenna
for Communications

A novel compact planar monopole antenna for Ultra Wide Band (UWB) applications is proposed that achieves sufficient impedance bandwidth for a VSWR less than 2 for frequencies from 3–15 GHz covering the entire UWB range (3.1–10.6 GHz). Also this design achieves good gain, constant group delay and a near omnidirectional radiation pattern over the UWB band. The UWB characteristics of the antenna are evaluated in frequency and time domains and the results are verified experimentally. . .

September 30, 2019

A New Modeling Methodology for Multipath Parameter
Estimation in UWB Channels

A simple, tractable, easy to implement and accurate solution is proposed for estimation of the parameters of the underlying multipath structure in an ultra wideband (UWB) indoor propagation channel based on a measurement campaign data available for a typical modern laboratory building. The proposed model establishes a normative to compare and evaluate performances between different UWB propagation models in realistic environments.
